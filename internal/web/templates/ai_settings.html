How the assistant works

Describe what you want in plain language. The assistant reads your current configuration, then builds the change step by step — creating backends, servers, frontends, listeners and routes in the right order — and validates the result with HAProxy's own checker, repairing anything that fails. It only ever stages changes; nothing goes live until you open Review & apply and press Save & Apply, which itself validates and rolls back on failure. Every change the assistant makes is recorded in the audit log under your account.
